antimatter

During the derivation of his equation, Dirac found that his equation produced twice the amount of expected electron states, that somehow there were electrons with a positive charge. He initially dismissed the thought of a positive electron, with no other options he finally accepted that there were anti-electrons or positrons as they are recognized today.
